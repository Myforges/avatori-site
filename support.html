Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate an avatar?

Open Avatori, tap the photo button, choose a clear photo of your face, pick a style, and tap Generate. Results are usually ready in moments.

Why isn't my photo working?

For best results: use a photo with one clearly visible face, good lighting, and minimal occlusion (no large sunglasses, hats covering your face, etc.). Group photos are not supported in this version.

Where are my photos stored?

Your photo is processed in memory only — it is never written to persistent storage on our servers, and is discarded immediately after processing completes. Generated avatars are stored only on your device. We never use your photos to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I save or share my avatar?

Tap the share icon on the result screen. You can save to Photos or share via the standard iOS share sheet.

How do refunds work?

All in-app purchases are processed by Apple, and refunds are issued by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow the steps. We're happy to help if Apple denies your request — email us with your transaction ID.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone > tap your name at the top > Subscriptions > tap Avatori > Cancel Subscription.

Can I use Avatori on iPad?

Avatori is designed for iPhone. It will run on iPad in compatibility mode, but the layout is optimized for iPhone.

What can I NOT do with Avatori?

Please read our Terms of Service. Briefly: no minors, no celebrities or identifiable people without consent, no sexually explicit content, no harmful or deceptive use.

Is my data sold to advertisers?

No. We do not sell your data. We do not use your photos for advertising.

How do I delete my data?

Source photos are processed in memory only and are never stored on our servers. To request deletion of any other data we may hold (such as anonymous usage logs), email support@myforges.com.

The App crashed or generated a weird result. What do I do?

Email support@myforges.com with: your iOS version, App version, what you were trying to do, and (if possible) a screenshot. AI-generated content can sometimes produce unexpected output — sorry about that. We're continuously improving the models.
