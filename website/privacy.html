Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026 — Effective: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyForges ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Avatori mobile application ("App") and related website at avatori.myforges.com.

By using Avatori, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos You Upload

When you choose a photo to generate an AI avatar, the photo is uploaded to our servers for processing. We process the photo solely to produce the requested avatar.

1.2 Generated Content

The AI-generated avatars are returned to your device. We do not permanently store your generated avatars on our servers.

1.3 Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive a transaction receipt to verify your purchase, but we do not receive your payment card details. Receipt validation is handled via RevenueCat.

1.4 Device and Usage Information

We collect anonymous, aggregate technical information such as device model, iOS version, App version, crash logs, and feature usage. This data does not personally identify you.

1.5 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address unless you contact support.
  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, microphone, or location.

2. How We Use Your Photos

  • To generate the AI avatar you requested.
  • To monitor for prohibited content (see Terms of Service) using automated tools.

We do not:

  • Use your photos to train AI models.
  • Share your photos with advertisers.
  • Sell your photos to third parties.
  • Use your photos for marketing without explicit consent.

6. Your Rights

6.1 GDPR (EU/UK Users)

If you reside in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

The legal basis for our processing is your consent (uploading a photo) and our legitimate interest in providing the service.

6.2 CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of any "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

6.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@myforges.com. We will respond within 30 days.
